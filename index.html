I have been many things: an EMT, a server, a sales representative, and arguably a pseudo-therapist to friends, acquaintances, and strangers. Okay, seriously now, I have many different experiences from these work experiences that range from patient care and strict adherence to protocols, to customer service, team-building and active listening. One interesting fact about me is I lived on a yoga ashram for a month. It changed my perception of how I should be thinking, working, and living; to work and live without expectations of praise and rewards, but for the sake of the experiences. I chose to take an intensive course on programming after I finished a course on computers at Atlantic Cape Community College. I have always loved computers, but never had the opportunity to take a class to discover and unlock this latent passion. Any time I can delve deeply into understanding the structure of how something works, I find satisfaction. After taking that class I decided I would begin a programmming/coding bootcamp at Epicodus before I start my bachelor's degree in computer science.
